== English version ==

You are reading this as you were redirected to this page from my LinkedIn profile you have just visited.
You are likely to be a recruiter or headhunter.
You may have sent an invitation to me to join your network.
You may have sent an inMail to me, waiting for my answer.

Please do read this till the end. You will save your time, this of your colleagues, your company and your acquaintainces.
Here is what you need to know:

1) I am not currently looking for a job in an active way
2) My LinkedIn profile is presumed to always be up-to-date
3) These days I go to LinkedIn twice a week
4) I only accept invitations from people I have already met in real life, phoned to or had a video chat with.
5) I systematically decline invitations from people I don't know
6) I am fed up with (French) IT contracting or consulting companies
7) I am very unlikely to be the candidate you are looking for, in case you offer me a job
8) Never expect a quick reply from me (see point 3)

Thank you for having read this. Think twice before contacting me and do it only if you have a good reason! And why not going around my GitHub repository if you feel like it!
